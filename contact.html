Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can we implement the AI Care Planner?

Implementation typically takes 4-6 weeks, including integration with your existing systems, staff training, and initial customization for your specific protocols.

Is the AI Care Planner compliant with healthcare regulations?

Yes, our solution is designed to be HIPAA compliant and meets all relevant healthcare data security standards. We provide documentation to support your compliance requirements.

Can the system integrate with our existing EHR?

Yes, we offer integration capabilities with major EHR systems including Epic, Cerner, Allscripts, and others. Our team will work with your IT department to ensure smooth data exchange.

How is the AI trained and updated?

Our AI is initially trained on a vast dataset of wound care outcomes and medical literature. It receives quarterly updates based on new research and continuously improves from anonymized outcome data.
